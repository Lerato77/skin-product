What is Organic Skincare?
Organic skincare products are made using ingredients sourced from natural, organically grown plants without synthetic chemicals, pesticides, or harmful additives. These products aim to nourish skin naturally while minimizing environmental impact and promoting skin health through pure botanicals and essential oils.
