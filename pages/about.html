About Us

Welcome to Lirah's GlowEssence — where nature meets science to bring out the best in your skin.

Founded in 2014, Lirah’s glow organics is a boutique skincare company based in Polokwane, South Africa. The company started with handmade organic soaps and quickly grew into a full range of natural, cruelty-free skincare products. Lirah’s glow sources ingredients locally, supports sustainable farming, and is committed to zero-waste packaging.

Thank you for trusting us on your skincare journey. Let's glow together!
